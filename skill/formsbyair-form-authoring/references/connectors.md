# Connector catalog

Integrations available in the FormsByAir platform, from the connector
projects in the private FormsByAir.Web repository (July 2026 snapshot).
Use this to answer "can FormsByAir integrate with X?" — for how a specific
connector is configured, the subscription settings live in the platform,
not the form XSD (forms reference subscriptions only by `subscriptionid`).

## Generic delivery & transport

HTTP, Rest, WebHook, HTMLForm, Email, SMTP2GO, SFTP, FileSystem,
Dropbox, Google (incl. Google Sheets), Microsoft, Azure, Amazon

## CRM & sales

Salesforce, Dynamics365, DynamicsCRM, HubSpot, Pipedrive, PipelineDeals,
SuiteCRM, LessAnnoyingCRM, ActiveCampaign, MailChimp, Monday, Trello,
VaultRE (real estate CRM)

## Identity verification & AML/KYC

Cloudcheck (incl. biometric/ID scan), VIXVerify, GetVerified, Equifax,
Refinitiv (screening), CreditWorks, CreditorWatch

## NZ/AU registries & data services

MBIE (NZ Companies Office), ABR (Australian Business Register), ANZSIC
(industry classification), CharitiesServices, CarJam (NZ vehicle data),
NZPost (address data), AddressFinder, Addy, Mastersoft, Addressable,
LinkMarketServices, NZXWT

## Payments

Stripe, Bambora, PaymentExpress (Windcave)

## Wealth, finance & industry platforms

Adminis (wealth administration — see `assets/example-integration-map.json`),
APLY, Alphatec, Consilium, CraigsInvestmentPartners, Elite, finPOWER,
LMIGroup (insurance), LegalOffice, MMC, TrusteesExecutors

## Documents, comms & analytics

Docusign (e-signature), Twilio (SMS), PowerBI, Cliniko (health practice)
